Linux
Most loved and powerful operating system in the world ever. Made by Linus Torvalds. Completely free and Open-Source for everyone. Offers Insane customisability and Privacy. Harder to learn for Beginners
Windows
Most used operating system in the world and owned by Microsoft, one of the richest companies in the world. Orignally founded by Bill Gates. Easy to use Interface and decent customisability. Low Privacy
MacOS
Most loved UI/UX OS experience due to to simplicity and modern Interface. Owned by Apple and Founded by Steve Jobs. Has low customisability and lower privacy than linux
TempleOS
Most respected OS in the world. Made by Terry Davis alone entirely despite facing tough mental health challenges. Simple Interface and Max Privacy, but biased religious stuff and outdated UI and no internet support
